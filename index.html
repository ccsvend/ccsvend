Frequently Asked Questions

What payment methods do your machines accept?

Our machines accept all major payment forms including cash, credit/debit cards, mobile payments (Apple Pay, Google Pay), and contactless transactions for maximum convenience.

How often do you restock the machines?

We typically restock weekly, but this can be customized based on your workplace's traffic and consumption patterns. High-traffic locations may receive more frequent visits.

What happens if the machine breaks down?

We provide full maintenance and repair services. Current customers receive priority support with response times within one hour during business hours.

Can we customize the product selection?

Absolutely! We work with you to stock products that match your team's preferences and dietary requirements. Product selection can be adjusted based on sales data and feedback.

Do you serve areas outside Broward County?

Currently, we focus our services on Broward County, Florida to ensure we can provide the best possible service and response times to our customers.

What if our employee count changes?

We understand businesses grow and change. We can discuss adjustments to service based on changing needs and traffic patterns at your location.
